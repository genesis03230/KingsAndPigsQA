## TC-01: Complete Level After Collecting All Required Items (Happy Path)

**Test Type:** Manual  
**Test Level:** Gameplay / Functional  
**Priority:** High  

### Preconditions
- Game launched successfully
- Player has entered a playable level
- Collectible items are present in the level
- Level exit is visible but initially locked or inactive

### Steps
1. Start the level
2. Collect all required items placed in the level
3. Navigate the player character to the level exit
4. Interact with the exit to complete the level

### Expected Result
- The level exit becomes available only after all required items are collected
- The level transition occurs successfully
- Game progression advances to the next scene or level
